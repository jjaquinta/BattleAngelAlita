Gally looked around at her trembling face. Not a single person was there to take her side. "That doubting face of yours, it’ll soothe my heart pain for sure!" The bull-headed man literally threw out his iron fist, and that clenched fist was about as big as Gally’s head. "Brace yourself, baby! Heavenly Knockout Punch!!" The bull-headed man stepped in with his right leg from his right-side stance and followed through by pushing his shoulder forward, thrusting out his whole right arm. Despite his foolish demeanor, his straightforward punch landed accurately, crouching low and drawing Gally in.
